Hardware Needed:

Display Screen:
- RPi Touchscreen

PWM Fan Control:
- MOSFET to vary voltage based off PWM signal from Arduino
- 12V Fans (two radiator fans on my car)

Traction Control:
- Inputs: 
    - VR to Hall Effect Sensor Converters
    - Engine RPM Input (used to calculate gear)
- Outputs:
    - Analog Signal to ECU between 0 and 5V indicating the amount to retard the ignition/boost cut
